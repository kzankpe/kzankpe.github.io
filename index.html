About Me

Devops Engineer

I am Devops Engineer at Thales Services Numeriques, a company specialized in the design, development and maintenance of secure Information Systems. I graduated in 2007 Telecommunication Engineer from ISEP (Institut Superieur d'Electronique de Paris). As student, I worked at Numericable and ANSSI (National Security Agency of Information Systems) from 2009 to 2012.

After two years in Israel as Field Services Engineer for Thomson Broadcast (Arelis Group), I have decided to work my way to cloud technologies. I began my cloud journey, in 2016, at Ariane Systems as Technical Implementation Manager and then joined Neocase Software as Cloud Administrator in 2018. Within those two companies, I enriched my DevOps skills such as Infrastructure as Code, Continuous integration, Continuous Delivery ...

I value Growth through knowledge and I always look for opportunities to improve myself. Openness to change and willingness to adapt, those traits help me to achieve my goals. I am passionate about sports, I enjoy running, boxing and football.
